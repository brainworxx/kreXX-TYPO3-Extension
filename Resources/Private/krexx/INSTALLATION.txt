Manual installation
===================
1.) Upload the whole kreXX directory to your webserver. Put it somewhere,
    where you are able to include it to your project.
2.) Include as early as possible the file kreXX.php into your project.
    Normally this is the index.php.

Using composer
==============
composer require brainworxx/krexx
Our composer page can be found here:
https://packagist.org/packages/brainworxx/krexx
